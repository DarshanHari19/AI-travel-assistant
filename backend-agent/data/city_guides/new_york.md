# Strategic Travel Guide: New York City

## Seasonal Climate Patterns
* **January - March:** High risk of "Nor'easters." These storms cause massive cancellations at LGA due to short runways and crosswinds.
* **July - August:** Extreme humidity and "Heat Island" effect. Afternoon thunderstorms are frequent and cause 1-2 hour ground stops daily at JFK.

## Business Intelligence
* **Airport Strategy:** If EWR (Newark) reports visibility below 1 mile, switch to Amtrak for North-East corridor travel; EWR is the first to bottleneck in the tri-state area.
* **Commute Logic:** Heavy rain causes immediate gridlock in Midtown. Use the Subway (Lettered/Numbered lines) as they are less affected by surface-level flooding than the bus network.
