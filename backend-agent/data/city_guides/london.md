# Strategic Travel Guide: London

## Seasonal Climate Patterns
* **March - May:** Transitional. Frequent "sun showers." Highs of 12°C, but wind chill from the Thames makes it feel like 5°C.
* **October - December:** High fog density. This historically leads to "Category III" approach conditions at Heathrow (LHR), causing significant morning flight stacks.

## Business Intelligence
* **Transport Strategy:** Avoid the "Heathrow Express" during heavy rain; track signaling often fails. Use the Elizabeth Line for more modern, weather-resilient infrastructure.
* **The "Black Cab" Rule:** In sudden downpours, Uber surge pricing in the City (Financial District) can exceed 3x. Standard Black Cabs are metered and more reliable during storms.
